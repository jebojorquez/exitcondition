Here you'll find a collection of both visual and audio work collected over the past 35 years. I perform my musical endeavors as Sleep of Eons

Collected Works

VISUAL

Most of the static and video imagery I use is produced with digital and analog hardware, the Processing programming language, oscilloscope graphics, VHS video cameras, mobile apps and other sources of optical corruption. I have a fancy for feedback and fractals and anthing geometric. I am greatly inspired by and indebted to the respective mind bending and whimsical works of Andrei Jay and Daniel Shiffman.

AUDIO

Primarily moudular synth based sounds. Distortion and feedback are key elements but the basis of most compositions are self generative systems that react and modulate based on chance and probability. Recorded usually dirctly to Ableton Live or often to 4track cassette for further manipulation
